Andrew Brinkworth

Andrew Brinkworth is a NASM Certified Personal Trainer and Nutrition Coach dedicated to helping individuals achieve their fitness goals through a holistic approach that combines mindset coaching, nutrition, and effective resistance training.

With a Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology from Temple University, Andrew brings a strong educational foundation to his training practice. His strengths lie in mindset coaching, nutrition coaching, hypertrophy-based resistance training, circuit-style boot camps, and maximizing the use of diverse equipment.

Andrew has been fortunate to work with a wide demographic, making him a versatile asset in helping individuals meet their goals—no matter their experience level or history. As a 1:1 coach, he is an effective communicator, intentional listener, and compassionate collaborator.

His intentionality is his greatest strength, as his passion is for connecting with and helping people. Andrew believes in playing the long game, because health lasts a lifetime.

My Coaching Philosophy

I believe that any goals I try to visualize in my imagination are only a fraction of what I am capable of achieving with hard work and consistency. This is a mantra I try to instill in those who are interested in improving their mindset, and are ready to build a lifestyle centered around success.

My strengths lie in mindset, nutrition coaching, hypertrophy-based resistance training, circuit-style boot camps, and making the most of as many pieces of equipment as I can. I have been lucky to work with a wide demographic, making me a versatile asset to helping individuals meet their goals—no matter their experience or history.

As a 1:1 Coach, I believe I am an effective communicator, intentional listener, and compassionate collaborator. My intentionality is my greatest strength, as my passion is for connecting with and helping people. I also believe in playing the long game, because health lasts a lifetime.
