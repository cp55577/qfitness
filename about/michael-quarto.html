Michael Quarto

Founder and Owner, Q Fitness

As a Physical Therapist Assistant (PTA) and healthcare professional, Michael Quarto brings a medically informed approach to safe strength training for adults and athletes at our 24-hour gym in West Chester, PA. Michael established Q Fitness in 1999 as one of the first 24-hour gyms in the Tri-State area, combining his healthcare background with practical fitness experience.

With over 30 years of sports training experience and more than 100,000 documented training hours since 1994, Michael has built ten Training Centers throughout Delaware and Chester Counties. Throughout his career, he has worked with thousands of clients ranging from fitness beginners to professional athletes in MLB, NHL, and the NFL.
