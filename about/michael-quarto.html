Michael Quarto - PTA, National Powerlifting Record Holder, and Your Fitness Guide

Michael Quarto, PTA

With over 30 years of sports training experience, Michael Quarto founded Q Fitness in 1999 to provide West Chester with a premier 24/7 fitness destination. He has been providing Motivation, Accountability, and Experienced/Applied Knowledge since 1994.

As a Physical Therapist Assistant (PTA) and healthcare professional, Michael brings a unique medical perspective to fitness training, ensuring proper form and technique to prevent injury while maximizing results. He has trained many professional athletes from a variety of sports to help them prevent injury, get faster, stronger, and maintain mental toughness.

His credentials include being a National powerlifting record holder, demonstrating his commitment to strength training excellence and his ability to help clients achieve their most ambitious fitness goals. He has over 30 years of sports training experience and has worked with professional athletes across multiple sports.

Michael's approach combines his healthcare background with practical fitness experience, creating personalized programs that address individual needs while maintaining the highest standards of safety and effectiveness. He specializes in improving Speed, Agility, Strength and Conditioning to maximize your full athletic potential.
