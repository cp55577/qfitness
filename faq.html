Everything you need to know about Q Fitness

Q Fitness is open 24/7 for members! We were the first 24-hour gym in the Tri-State area since 1999. Members can access the gym anytime with their secure key card. Staff is available for personal training and group classes by appointment.

You can book personal training sessions or group classes by calling us at (610) 918-9200 or emailing info@qfitness.com. We'll work with you to find the best times that fit your schedule.

We offer three membership tiers: Basic ($30/month) with 24/7 gym access, Premium ($50/month) including group classes and nutrition consultation, and Elite ($80/month) with personal training sessions and custom workout plans. All memberships include secure entry and video surveillance.

We have 20 cardio machines, 40 custom weight machines, a complete free weight area, TRX suspension training, a 300 lb heavy bag, and an outdoor training area. Our facility is equipped with everything you need for a complete workout.

Michael Quarto is our founder and head trainer. He's a Physical Therapist Assistant (PTA) with over 25 years of experience, a National powerlifting record holder, and a healthcare professional. He founded Q Fitness in 1999 and brings a unique medical perspective to fitness training.
