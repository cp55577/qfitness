Our Story

Q Fitness was the first 24-hour gym in the Tri-State area since its 1999 small pilot training center in Newtown Square. Since then, Q Fitness's owner, Michael Quarto, has developed ten gyms from Delaware County to Chester County in less than eight years—not bad for starting as a shoestring business!
